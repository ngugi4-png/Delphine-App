Import Historical Daily Reports (CSV)
Upload a daily-sales CSV with columns: Date, Price Reading, Closing Price Reading, Petrol Sales, 2T QTY, 2T Kupima, 2T Sales, Used Oil QTY, Used Oil Sales, Shell Advance QTY, Amount, Total Sales Amount, Liters Reading, Closing Liter Reading, Total Liters Sold, Liters Sold, Sales Rate. Each row becomes one approved historical report for the selected branch. These reports have no cash-reconciliation data (deposits/till/float) — only sales, litres, and product quantities — so inventory and revenue figures will be accurate but the reconciliation section will show zeros for imported days.
